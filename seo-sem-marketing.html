Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our SEO / SEM Marketing service.

SEO (Search Engine Optimisation) is the process of improving your website's organic rankings in search results over time — without paying for clicks. SEM (Search Engine Marketing) uses paid ads like Google Ads to appear at the top of search results immediately. The most powerful strategy combines both — building long-term organic presence while using paid search to drive instant, targeted traffic.

SEO is a long-term investment. Most websites begin seeing meaningful improvements in rankings and traffic within 3 to 6 months of consistent work. Highly competitive industries may take longer, but the results compound over time — unlike paid ads, organic rankings continue delivering traffic without ongoing cost per click.
