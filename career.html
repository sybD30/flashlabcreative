Intern
Margao, Goa Full-time Fresher / Entry Level

FlashLab Creative is looking for enthusiastic, creative young individuals who are eager to learn and grow in a real agency environment. As an intern, you'll work alongside our senior team on live client campaigns — not make-believe projects. This is your chance to build a portfolio that actually matters, get mentored by industry professionals, and earn a fast-track to a full-time role.

What You'll Do
Graphic Design

Assist in creating visuals for social media, branding, print, and digital campaigns.

Video & Animation

Support the production team with editing, motion graphics, and content creation.

Web & Development

Contribute to web projects, landing pages, and app builds under senior supervision.

Social Media

Help manage client social accounts, plan content calendars, and write captions.

SEO Support

Learn and assist in keyword research, on-page optimisation, and reporting tasks.

Photography / Production

Join shoots, learn the production process, and assist with behind-the-scenes tasks.

Eligibility & Requirements
  • Willing to learn 100% — attitude matters more than qualifications
  • Enthusiastic, dedicated, and creative with a long-term mindset
  • Basic knowledge of any creative software (Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva, etc.) is a plus
  • Portfolio or any personal project work is appreciated but not mandatory
  • Stipend / Compensation based on skillset, department, and performance
  • Certificate + Letter of Recommendation on successful completion

● Got Questions?

Frequently
Asked Questions

We know the job-hunt can feel overwhelming — so here are answers to the most common questions we get. If you don't see your question here, just reach out and we'll help.

Contact Us
How do I apply for a position?
Select the role you're interested in from the "View Available Positions" section above, click "Apply Now", fill out the form, and submit. Our team will personally review your application and respond within 5 working days.
Do I need prior experience to apply?
Not necessarily. For our internship programme, we prioritise attitude, enthusiasm, and willingness to learn over formal experience. For senior roles, relevant experience and a portfolio are expected — but we always evaluate candidates holistically.
How long does the hiring process take?
We review applications within 5 working days. If shortlisted, you'll be invited for an interview or a short task assessment. The full process from application to offer typically takes 1–2 weeks.
Is the internship paid?
Internship stipends are based on the department, your skill level, and the scope of work. All details will be discussed during your interview. Top interns are regularly offered full-time positions.
Can I apply for multiple roles?
Yes, but we recommend applying for the role that best matches your primary skills. If you're versatile and feel you fit multiple departments, mention that in your "Why do you want to join?" section and we'll take note.
Where is the office located?
Our studio is located at G26 Vikrant Complex, Margao, Goa — 403601. All roles are primarily in-person at our Margao studio. We are Mon–Sat: 9am–6pm IST.
Do you offer a certificate for internships?
Yes! Every intern who successfully completes their programme receives an official Flashlab Creative Certificate of Completion and a Letter of Recommendation from our director — great for your portfolio and next interview.

Don't See Your
Role Listed?

We're always open to hearing from talented creatives — even if your specific role isn't listed yet. Tell us what you do best and we'll be in touch when the right opportunity opens up. We hire for passion as much as we hire for skill.
