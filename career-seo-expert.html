Flashlab is on a hunt for creative & enthusiastic young individuals — interns and professional minds willing to learn & cultivate their thoughts and ideas into creating the highest ranking on the internet through SEO strategies. ● The Role

What You'll Do

Key areas you'll be contributing to as an SEO Expert at FlashLab Creative.

Keyword Research

Identify high-impact keywords and search opportunities to drive relevant organic traffic for our clients.

On-Page SEO

Optimise meta tags, headings, content structure, and internal linking to maximise page authority.

Link Building

Develop and execute ethical backlink strategies to grow domain authority across client websites.

Analytics & Reporting

Track, analyse, and report on organic performance using Google Analytics and Search Console.

Technical SEO

Audit and resolve technical issues — crawlability, site speed, schema, and Core Web Vitals.

Local SEO

Help Goa-based and regional businesses dominate local search results and Google Business Profiles.
